    About

    Follow the Brick is a repetitive memory game where you follow the demonstrated color sequence until you make a mistake. The longer the sequence you repeat, the greater your score.

    The name Simon is a registered trademark owned by Milton-Bradley. And that is why I call this Follow the Brick.
